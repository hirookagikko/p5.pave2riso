PathRemoveHoles() removes all holes from a path, keeping only the outer contours.

Use case: After applying PathOffset() to text, you may want to fill the holes of letters like 'e', 'o', 'a', 'd', etc.

How it works:

  1. Analyzes the winding direction (CW/CCW) of each sub-path
  2. Keeps only clockwise (solid) contours
  3. Removes counter-clockwise (hole) contours
