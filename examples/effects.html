Plates

p5.pave2riso supports three types of post-processing effects to add classic print aesthetics to your designs:

Filter: Image processing effects like blur, threshold, and posterize using p5.js filters.

Halftone: Classic print dot patterns with customizable dot size, spacing, and angle. Perfect for achieving that authentic Risograph look.

Dither: Dithering algorithms (like Bayer) that create textured patterns from gradients, adding organic print character.

Effects can be applied to any fill or stroke type, and multiple effects can be combined for unique results.
